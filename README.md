# FedEx Supply Chain Analytics: Delivery Performance & Cost Optimization

## Project Overview

This project analyzes FedEx Logistics shipment data to understand supply chain performance, delivery efficiency, transportation costs, and vendor operations. The objective is to identify patterns affecting logistics performance and generate actionable business insights that can help improve operational efficiency and reduce costs.

## Business Problem

FedEx manages shipments across multiple countries, vendors, and transportation modes. Delays in delivery, increasing freight costs, and inefficient logistics planning can impact customer satisfaction and business performance. This project aims to analyze shipment data and identify opportunities for optimization.

## Dataset Information

* Total Records: 10,324
* Total Features: 33
* Domain: Logistics & Supply Chain Management

The dataset contains information related to:

* Shipment details
* Delivery schedules
* Freight costs
* Shipment modes
* Vendor agreements
* Product information
* Manufacturing sites
* Destination countries

## Key Insights

* Air transport was the most frequently used shipment mode.
* Freight cost generally increased with shipment weight.
* A small number of countries accounted for a significant portion of shipments.
* Delivery performance varied across shipment modes and vendor agreements.
* Opportunities exist to optimize transportation costs and improve delivery efficiency.

## Business Recommendations

* Improve monitoring of delayed shipments.
* Optimize shipment mode selection based on cost and urgency.
* Strengthen collaboration with high-performing vendors.
* Reduce transportation costs through better route and load planning.
* Implement real-time shipment tracking and performance monitoring.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be applied to logistics and supply chain operations to improve decision-making, reduce costs, and enhance delivery performance. The insights generated can help organizations optimize logistics strategies and improve customer satisfaction.
